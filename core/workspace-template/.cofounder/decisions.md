# Decisions Log

Key decisions made by the Cofounder orchestrator (or the founder, recorded
by the orchestrator) that shape how the company operates. This is a log of
*decisions*, not routine task completions — the Hermes Kanban board already
tracks task-level activity. A decision is anything a human cofounder would
remember and reference later ("didn't we already decide X?").

Format: one row per decision, newest last (append-only).

| Date | Decision | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| 2026-07-02 | Chose weekly digest over daily emails for support triage | Founder said daily was noisy; weekly keeps signal without fatigue |
